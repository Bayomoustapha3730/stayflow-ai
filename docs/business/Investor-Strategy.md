# Investor Strategy

## Purpose

This document captures the investor narrative, funding strategy, and evidence needed to support fundraising conversations for StayFlow AI.

## Investor Narrative

StayFlow AI addresses the operational burden of guest communication for short-stay hosts and property managers by combining WhatsApp, structured property knowledge, and AI concierge automation.

## Proof Points to Build

- Clear customer pain and willingness to pay.
- Pilot results showing faster response times or reduced manual workload.
- Strong retention from early property managers.
- Repeatable onboarding process.
- Responsible AI and security architecture.
- Scalable economics around messaging and AI usage.

## Funding Use Cases

- Product development.
- WhatsApp and AI integration maturity.
- Customer acquisition and onboarding.
- Security, compliance, and operational readiness.
- Support and customer success capacity.

## Investor Questions to Prepare For

- Why this market and why now?
- What makes StayFlow AI different from generic chatbots?
- How will the business control AI and messaging costs?
- What is the path to repeatable sales?
- How is guest and company data protected?

## Future Work

Develop a pitch deck, financial model, customer case studies, and market sizing analysis when early validation data is available.
