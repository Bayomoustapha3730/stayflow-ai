# Revenue Model

## Purpose

This document describes potential revenue streams, pricing drivers, and cost considerations for StayFlow AI.

## Revenue Streams

- Monthly subscriptions for hosts and property managers.
- Tiered plans based on property count or message volume.
- Premium features such as analytics, service request automation, or advanced AI workflows.
- Marketplace or service coordination fees where applicable.
- Setup or onboarding fees for larger property managers.

## Pricing Drivers

- Number of managed properties.
- Guest conversation volume.
- WhatsApp messaging costs.
- AI usage costs.
- Number of team members.
- Access to analytics, marketplace, payments, or automation features.

## Cost Structure

- Cloud hosting and database operations.
- WhatsApp Cloud API usage.
- OpenAI usage.
- Email and notification delivery.
- Support, onboarding, sales, and customer success.
- Payment processing fees where payments are enabled.

## Margin Considerations

The revenue model should account for variable messaging and AI costs. High-usage customers may need usage-based limits, overage pricing, or plan-based fair usage policies.

## Future Work

Validate pricing through interviews, pilots, willingness-to-pay testing, and cost modeling.
