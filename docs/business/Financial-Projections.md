# Financial Projections

## Purpose

This document defines the structure for StayFlow AI financial projections. Numbers should be updated as pricing, usage, costs, and customer acquisition assumptions become validated.

## Projection Inputs

- Number of customers.
- Average properties per customer.
- Average revenue per account.
- Monthly churn.
- WhatsApp usage per property.
- AI usage per conversation.
- Hosting and database costs.
- Support and onboarding costs.
- Sales and marketing spend.

## Key Metrics

- Monthly recurring revenue.
- Annual recurring revenue.
- Gross margin.
- Customer acquisition cost.
- Customer lifetime value.
- Churn rate.
- Payback period.
- Average revenue per property.

## Scenario Planning

Maintain at least three scenarios:

- Conservative case.
- Base case.
- Growth case.

## Review Cadence

Financial projections should be reviewed after pilot results, pricing changes, major infrastructure changes, and new channel experiments.

## Notes

Early projections are assumptions, not facts. Mark unvalidated assumptions clearly and replace them with observed data over time.
