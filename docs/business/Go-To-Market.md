# Go To Market

## Purpose

This document captures the go-to-market strategy for launching and growing StayFlow AI.

## Initial Market

The initial focus is Airbnb hosts and short-stay property managers in Kenya who use WhatsApp as a primary channel for guest support.

## Beachhead Segment

The likely beachhead is property managers with multiple listings. They experience repeated guest questions, operational coordination burden, and stronger need for scalable support workflows.

## Acquisition Channels

- Direct outreach to property managers.
- Partnerships with local host communities.
- Airbnb and short-stay operator networks.
- Content marketing around guest automation and WhatsApp concierge workflows.
- Referrals from early customers.

## Sales Motion

- Founder-led discovery and demos.
- Pilot programs with selected hosts or property managers.
- Guided onboarding for property knowledge setup.
- Case studies based on response time, workload reduction, and guest satisfaction.

## Launch Milestones

- Validate customer pain and willingness to pay.
- Run pilot deployments.
- Measure guest response quality and operational savings.
- Convert pilots into paid accounts.
- Build repeatable onboarding and support processes.
