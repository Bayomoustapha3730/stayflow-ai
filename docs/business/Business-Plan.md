# Business Plan

## Purpose

This document outlines the business plan for StayFlow AI and should be updated as customer discovery, market validation, and financial assumptions evolve.

## Business Summary

StayFlow AI is an AI-powered WhatsApp concierge platform for Airbnb hosts and property managers in Kenya. The platform helps hosts automate guest support, organize property knowledge, coordinate service requests, and improve guest experience through familiar messaging channels.

## Target Customers

- Individual Airbnb hosts managing one or a few listings.
- Property managers operating multiple short-stay units.
- Hospitality operators who rely on WhatsApp for guest communication.
- Service teams coordinating cleaning, maintenance, and guest support.

## Core Value Proposition

- Faster guest responses.
- Reduced repetitive support workload.
- Better consistency across property instructions.
- AI-assisted answers grounded in property knowledge.
- Operational visibility for property managers.

## Key Assumptions

- WhatsApp remains the primary guest communication channel in the target market.
- Hosts will pay for tools that reduce manual guest support.
- Property knowledge quality is central to AI response quality.
- Multi-property operators need team workflows, auditability, and reporting.

## Open Questions

- Which customer segment has the strongest willingness to pay?
- What onboarding support is required for hosts to adopt the platform?
- Which integrations are required before commercial launch?
