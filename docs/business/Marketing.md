# Marketing

## Purpose

This document captures marketing positioning, messaging, and channel ideas for StayFlow AI.

## Positioning

StayFlow AI helps Airbnb hosts and property managers deliver fast, consistent guest support on WhatsApp using AI-powered property knowledge.

## Messaging Themes

- Never miss a guest question.
- Turn property knowledge into instant WhatsApp answers.
- Reduce repetitive host work.
- Give guests reliable check-in, house rule, emergency, and local recommendation support.
- Scale guest communication across multiple properties.

## Content Ideas

- Guides for automating Airbnb guest support.
- WhatsApp concierge examples.
- Check-in and house rule communication templates.
- Case studies for property managers.
- Comparisons of manual guest support versus AI-assisted workflows.

## Marketing Channels

- LinkedIn outreach.
- Local host and property manager communities.
- Referral programs.
- Educational blog content.
- Short demo videos.
- Partnerships with service providers in the short-stay ecosystem.

## Brand Considerations

Marketing should feel practical, trustworthy, local, and operations-focused rather than overly futuristic.
