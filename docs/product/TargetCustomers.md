# Target Customers

## Purpose

This document defines the primary customer segments StayFlow AI is designed to serve.

## Primary Segments

### Independent Airbnb Hosts

Hosts managing one or a small number of short-stay listings.

Needs:

- Faster guest responses.
- Simple property setup.
- Affordable automation.
- Reduced repetitive communication.

### Multi-Property Hosts

Hosts operating several listings who need more consistent workflows.

Needs:

- Centralized property information.
- Better guest communication coverage.
- Cross-property visibility.
- Repeatable onboarding for new listings.

### Property Management Companies

Professional teams managing short-stay properties for owners.

Needs:

- Team access and role management.
- Guest conversation tracking.
- Service request coordination.
- Reporting and operational oversight.
- Strong company data isolation.

## Secondary Segments

- Hospitality operators using WhatsApp for guest support.
- Service teams coordinating cleaning, maintenance, and guest requests.
- Property owners who need visibility into manager performance.

## Buyer Considerations

Target customers are likely to evaluate StayFlow AI based on response speed, setup effort, guest satisfaction, reliability, pricing, and trust in AI-assisted communication.
