# Functional Requirements

## Purpose

This document captures the core product functions StayFlow AI should provide.

## Company and User Management

- Create and manage company profiles.
- Manage users, roles, and permissions.
- Support secure authentication and current-user context.
- Preserve company isolation across workflows.

## Property Management

- Create, update, list, view, and soft delete properties.
- Manage amenities, house rules, emergency contacts, recommendations, and knowledge articles.
- Search and paginate property lists.
- Audit property changes.

## Guest and Conversation Management

- Create and manage guest profiles.
- Link guests to conversations and properties.
- Track inbound and outbound WhatsApp messages.
- Support AI-assisted responses and human escalation.

## Marketplace and Service Requests

- Manage service providers.
- Create and track service requests.
- Assign and update operational work.
- Notify relevant users or guests when needed.

## Payments

- Track payment records.
- Support payment provider callbacks.
- Reconcile payment status.
- Protect sensitive payment data.

## Analytics

- Provide company and property-level metrics.
- Track conversation volume, response time, service request status, and AI activity.
- Support operational dashboards for property managers.
