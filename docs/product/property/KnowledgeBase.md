# Knowledge Base

## Purpose

The property knowledge base stores structured articles that help the AI concierge answer guest questions accurately.

## Article Examples

- Check-in instructions.
- Wi-Fi details.
- Parking instructions.
- Appliance usage.
- Backup power instructions.
- Water supply guidance.
- Building access.
- Checkout procedure.
- Troubleshooting steps.

## Product Requirements

- Knowledge articles should belong to a property and company.
- Each article should have a clear title and content body.
- Knowledge articles should be easy to create, update, and review.
- AI workflows should use only active knowledge articles.
- Changes to knowledge articles should be auditable.

## Content Guidance

Articles should be specific, current, and written in a way that can be safely reused in guest responses. Avoid mixing unrelated topics in one article.

## Quality Criteria

- Clear title.
- Accurate instructions.
- No secrets or sensitive internal-only information.
- Updated when property operations change.
- Reviewed before AI concierge use.

## Future Enhancements

- Article categories.
- Internal-only versus guest-visible flags.
- AI readiness score.
- Last-reviewed timestamp.
- Source attribution and version history.
