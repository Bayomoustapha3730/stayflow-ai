# Emergency Contacts

## Purpose

Emergency contacts provide guests and operators with property-specific contacts for urgent situations.

## Examples

- Security desk.
- Property manager.
- Maintenance contact.
- Medical emergency contact.
- Building supervisor.
- Local emergency service reference.

## Product Requirements

- Each emergency contact should include a name, role, and phone number.
- Emergency contacts should be scoped to a property.
- Emergency contacts should be easy to update.
- AI concierge workflows should use emergency contacts carefully and only in relevant contexts.
- Inactive or deleted contacts should not be shown or used.

## Safety Guidance

Emergency information must be accurate and current. Product workflows should encourage regular review of emergency contacts.

## Future Enhancements

- Emergency contact priority order.
- Contact availability hours.
- Escalation instructions.
- Last-reviewed timestamp.
- Emergency-only visibility controls.
