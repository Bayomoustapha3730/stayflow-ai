# Vision

## Purpose

This document captures the long-term product vision for StayFlow AI.

## Vision Statement

StayFlow AI aims to become the trusted AI concierge operating layer for short-stay hosts and property managers in Kenya, helping every guest receive fast, accurate, and helpful support through familiar messaging channels.

## Long-Term Direction

- Make guest communication faster and more reliable for hosts.
- Reduce repetitive operational work for property managers.
- Give guests accurate answers about properties, services, payments, emergencies, and local recommendations.
- Use AI responsibly with clear guardrails, auditability, and escalation paths.
- Build a platform that can grow from single-property hosts to multi-property management businesses.

## Product Principles

- Guest experience should feel helpful, local, and responsive.
- Host workflows should reduce manual coordination.
- AI should assist operations without hiding accountability.
- Tenant and company isolation must be preserved as the product scales.

## Review Cadence

Review the vision when the product enters a new market, adds a major integration, or changes its primary customer segment.
