# Pricing

## Purpose

This document captures pricing assumptions and packaging ideas for StayFlow AI.

## Pricing Goals

- Align pricing with value delivered to hosts and property managers.
- Support small hosts while allowing growth into larger property portfolios.
- Keep pricing understandable and easy to compare.
- Account for messaging, AI, infrastructure, and support costs.

## Potential Pricing Dimensions

- Number of properties.
- Monthly guest conversation volume.
- AI usage volume.
- Number of team members.
- Advanced features such as analytics, service marketplace, or payment workflows.

## Packaging Ideas

- **Starter**: Small hosts with limited properties and basic WhatsApp concierge support.
- **Growth**: Multi-property hosts needing team access, stronger automation, and reporting.
- **Professional**: Property managers needing service requests, analytics, and priority support.

## Cost Considerations

- WhatsApp message costs.
- AI provider costs.
- Database and hosting costs.
- Support and onboarding effort.
- Payment provider fees if payment workflows are included.

## Future Work

Pricing should be validated through customer discovery, competitive research, and usage-cost modeling.
