# Success Metrics

## Purpose

This document defines metrics used to evaluate StayFlow AI product success.

## Customer Adoption

- Number of active companies.
- Number of active properties.
- Property onboarding completion rate.
- Time to first property configured.
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate when pricing is active.

## Guest Experience

- Guest message response time.
- Percentage of guest questions resolved without human escalation.
- Guest satisfaction score when collected.
- Number of unresolved or stale conversations.

## Operational Efficiency

- Reduction in repetitive manual responses.
- Number of AI-assisted replies.
- Service request creation and resolution time.
- Escalation volume by property or company.

## Business Metrics

- Monthly recurring revenue.
- Average revenue per account.
- Churn rate.
- Customer acquisition cost.
- Gross margin after AI and messaging costs.

## Quality and Trust

- AI response correction rate.
- Failed WhatsApp message rate.
- Authentication and authorization incident count.
- Cross-company access incident count.
- Support tickets related to incorrect property information.

## Review Cadence

Metrics should be reviewed after pilots, releases, pricing changes, and major workflow launches.
