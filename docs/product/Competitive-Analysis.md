# Competitive Analysis

## Purpose

This document tracks market context and competitive positioning for StayFlow AI.

## Competitive Categories

- Property management systems.
- Guest messaging tools.
- WhatsApp automation platforms.
- AI chatbot and concierge products.
- Local service marketplace or vendor coordination tools.

## Differentiation Themes

- WhatsApp-first guest experience.
- Kenya-focused property management context.
- AI-assisted concierge workflows grounded in property knowledge.
- Multi-property operations with company isolation.
- Service request and marketplace potential.

## Evaluation Criteria

Compare competitors across:

- Ease of onboarding.
- Guest communication channels.
- AI quality and safety controls.
- Property knowledge management.
- Service request workflows.
- Pricing model.
- Local market fit.
- Security and auditability.

## Research Notes

Competitive analysis should include source dates, observed pricing, target customer segments, and product limitations. Avoid relying on stale claims without verification.
