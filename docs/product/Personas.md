# Personas

## Purpose

This document describes the primary users and stakeholders for StayFlow AI.

## Airbnb Host

An individual host managing one or a small number of listings.

- Wants faster guest communication.
- Needs simple property setup and clear automation.
- May not have dedicated operations staff.
- Values affordability, ease of use, and reliability.

## Property Manager

A professional manager responsible for multiple properties and guest stays.

- Needs centralized company and property management.
- Wants consistency across guest communication.
- Needs escalation, service request, payment, and reporting workflows.
- Values auditability, team access, and operational visibility.

## Guest

A traveler staying at a managed property.

- Wants quick answers through WhatsApp.
- Needs accurate check-in, house rule, emergency, and local recommendation information.
- Expects responses to be clear, polite, and timely.

## Operations Team Member

A staff member supporting guest requests, vendors, and property issues.

- Needs clear task ownership and service request status.
- Needs context from conversations and property knowledge.
- Values fast triage and escalation workflows.

## Business Owner

The decision-maker evaluating StayFlow AI for business value.

- Wants lower support workload.
- Needs confidence in privacy, security, and reliability.
- Values revenue protection, guest satisfaction, and operational scale.
