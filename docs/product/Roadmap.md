# Roadmap

## Purpose

This document outlines the product roadmap for StayFlow AI. It should be updated as discovery, implementation, and customer feedback evolve.

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Backend project setup.
- PostgreSQL data model.
- Company management.
- Authentication and authorization groundwork.
- Property domain and knowledge articles.
- Engineering and product documentation.

## Phase 2: Guest and Messaging

- Guest management.
- Conversation model and APIs.
- WhatsApp Cloud API webhook integration.
- Inbound and outbound message workflows.
- Basic AI concierge orchestration.

## Phase 3: Operations

- Service provider marketplace.
- Service request lifecycle.
- Notifications and escalation workflows.
- Payment records and provider integration.
- Analytics foundations.

## Phase 4: Intelligence and Scale

- Retrieval over property knowledge.
- AI evaluation and safety workflows.
- Multi-property insights.
- Background processing and event-driven workflows.
- Advanced monitoring and operational dashboards.

## Roadmap Notes

Roadmap items are directional and should be validated through customer discovery, technical feasibility, and business priority.
