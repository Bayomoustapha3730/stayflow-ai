# Conversations

## Purpose

The conversations experience supports guest messaging, AI concierge review, escalation, and operational follow-up.

## Primary Content

- Conversation timeline.
- Guest and property context.
- AI-generated replies.
- Human operator messages.
- Escalation status.
- Related service requests.
- Delivery and read states where available.

## UX Goals

- Make the latest guest need immediately clear.
- Distinguish AI and human responses.
- Provide safe controls for sending, editing, or approving messages.
- Surface property knowledge used in the response when useful.

## States

- Open conversation.
- Waiting for guest.
- AI draft available.
- Escalated to human.
- Resolved conversation.
- Failed outbound message.

## Future Considerations

Add conversation assignment, internal notes, AI confidence indicators, and quality review workflows as operations mature.
