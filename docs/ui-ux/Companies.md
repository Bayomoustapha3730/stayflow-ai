# Companies

## Purpose

The companies experience supports tenant setup, company profile management, team ownership, and administrative context.

## Primary Content

- Company name and legal name.
- Contact email and phone number.
- Country and time zone.
- Active or inactive status.
- Audit history where appropriate.

## UX Goals

- Make company identity and status obvious.
- Keep administrative forms clear and compact.
- Prevent accidental destructive actions.
- Explain soft delete or deactivation behavior.

## States

- Company list.
- Company detail.
- Create company.
- Edit company.
- Validation errors.
- Deactivated company.

## Future Considerations

Team management, billing, permissions, and tenant settings may later become tabs or related pages inside the company area.
