# Guests

## Purpose

The guests experience should help operators understand guest context, contact details, stay history, and support needs.

## Primary Content

- Guest name and phone number.
- Company and property context.
- Active conversations.
- Service requests.
- Payment or booking references where available.
- Notes or escalation flags.

## UX Goals

- Present guest context quickly during support interactions.
- Keep private information protected and purposeful.
- Make conversation and service history easy to scan.
- Support fast handoff between AI and human operators.

## States

- Guest list.
- Guest detail.
- Guest search.
- Unknown guest from inbound message.
- Guest with active issue.
- Guest with no conversation history.

## Future Considerations

Guest profiles may later include consent, communication preferences, and data retention controls.
